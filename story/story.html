Outdoor Education

Why is Outdoor Education Important?

A class trip an outdoor education center at any time of the year will surely be a highlight of the students' school year. Students will get to participate in activities that will help them build their confidence and teamwork skills. It will also provide the students with a unique experience that simply can't be achieved in the classroom.
